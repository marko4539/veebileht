I started tattooing in the fall of 2021. First, I did an internship at a studio, and then I did it independently for another year. I finally started working at Backbone tattoo studio in August 2022. I have considered becoming a tattoo artist all my life. I have been looking through tattoo books in bookstores since I was one year old, but actually, my first machine that I purchased was 9 years later through TikTok. I saw a video of a person tattooing faux leather and thought I'd like to try it too. Although I am afraid of blood and needles, I associate the process of tattooing with art, not a medical procedure. At the moment I do graphic tattoos, but in the future, I would like to do a more neo-traditional style. I love to tattoo plants and animals such as peonies and butterflies. I am interested in bigger projects. My hourly rate is 80€/h.

I bought my first graphics desk in 2017. I learned how to draw in Photoshop, but in the fall of 2018, I switched to an iPad and started drawing in Procreate, which I still use today. I get my motivation for my digital art from Youtube, especially from the creators who gained their followers because of their unique style. I mostly draw portraits on the iPad, which can also be ordered from me. The price is negotiable. My digital art style is the absolute opposite of my tattoo style - it's very colorful and detailed. Thanks to digital art, I reached 160,000 followers on YouTube and 30,000 on Instagram. In addition, I have collaborated with Nickelodeon.
